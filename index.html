Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We're happy to come out and assess your project at no charge. We'll give you honest recommendations and clear pricing before any work begins.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Metro Omaha and the surrounding areas, including Omaha, Council Bluffs, La Vista, Papillion, Bellevue, and nearby communities. If you're not sure if we cover your area, just give us a call!

How long does drywall repair typically take?

It depends on the scope of the project. Small patches can often be completed in a day, while larger repairs might take 2-3 days including drying time. We'll give you a clear timeline when we assess your specific situation.

Can you match my existing wall texture?

Absolutely! Texture matching is one of our specialties. We take pride in making repairs blend seamlessly with your existing walls, so you won't even be able to tell where the repair was done.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards. Payment is due upon completion of the work, and we'll provide you with a detailed invoice.

Are you insured?

Yes, we are fully insured. Our team is also OSHA 10-hour certified, so you can have complete peace of mind knowing your home is in professional, qualified hands.
